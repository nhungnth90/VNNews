GUIDE TO DOWNLOAD PDF

  • Step by step guide to downloading the PDF version of Viet Nam News’s daily, weekly and monthly publications.
  • 1. Go to the Viet Nam News homepage: https://vietnamnews.vnanet.vn or https://vietnamnews.vn or http://vietnamnews.com.vn
  • 2. Click onto E-Paper on the right hand side.
  • 3. Fill in the form provided and send it to vnspay@vnsmail.com.
    Note: Your default username is your registered email address. The password is chosen by you.
  • 4. After receiving your subscription form, the cashier will send you an invoice. You can pay in cash, by transfer or cards.
  • 5. As soon as your subscription fee is paid, the Administrator will activate your account and send you a confirmation email.
  • 6. To download the issues you want, select the date on the calendar and click the Download button.
  • 7. For customer support, please feel free to contact the Administrator at newsletter@vnsmail.com
